Let's recap

WordPress Translation Day 3 is over. The 24-hour global event dedicated to the translation of the WordPress ecosystem (core, themes, plugins) has proven to be, once again, a great success.

 

The numbers below are self explanatory, and considering the fully volunteer-based organisation (and resources) and the rather short time we can’t help but considering this a huge success and a well deserved celebration of the WordPress Polyglots Team, one that truly reflects the value and the positive energy of the entire Global WordPress Community, and that shines a special spotlight to the thousands of volunteers, worldwide, that offer their time and competence to ensure this powerful platform is available to as many people as possible.

 

The team will take a short break, recoup, breathe and get working on WordPress Translation Day 4. We will aim at creating an even bigger, wider and more interesting event, and at further helping the WordPress Polyglots Team grow and get better, so they can better fulfill their mission as user empowerers.

 

We – the organizers – want to thank you all. Your participation, your energy, your love, your enthusiasm is our propeller, and we invite you to stay tuned for the upcoming news.
