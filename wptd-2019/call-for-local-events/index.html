Call for Local Event Organizers

We would like to invite everyone involved with the Make WordPress Polyglots team to consider organizing or taking a part in a small local MeetUp dedicated to translating. This is what we call ‘local events’.

You can find a guide on how to organize a local event in the Polyglots Handbook. Please fill in the form below with when and where your local event will be and its details. We are looking forward to finding out about your event. To help us include your event on the website, please submit your form by 4 May 2019.
